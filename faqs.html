FAQS


Q - What operating systems does the CellWatch APP currently operate on?
A - The currently works on Android 5.1 & newer. The IOS version will be coming soon.

Q – How exactly does it work?
A – Simple, there are two APPs to install. One goes on the parent phone and the other on the child’s phone. The parent then manages the child’s phone at their discretion.

Q – Can the child just delete the APP?
A – NO. The APP on the child’s phone is password protected. And, if the child attempts to delete the APP, the parent is notified by text.

Q - If I use the room beacon to turn off my child’s phone at night (so he doesn’t text his friends all night), does he still have access to emergency numbers?
A – YES, the parent can add as many emergency contact numbers as desired and they will all work in the presence of the beacon.

Q – How does the location tracking feature work?
A – Every 5 minutes the exact location (per google maps) is recorded. You will have the past 3 days of tracking available to you at any moment.

Q – On the Parent APP, what is the “Set Security Code?”
A – The set security code is the password the parent chooses. It is necessary to remove the Child APP from the child’s phone once installed.

CellWatch is one of the leading parental assistance apps in the marketplace today. It helps keep the responsible parents informed as to their child’s technology usage.
