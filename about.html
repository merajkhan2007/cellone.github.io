About CellWatch

In today’s world, raising teenagers comes with some guaranteed discomforts. We often do not know where our children are, what they are doing and if they’ll tell us the truth should we ask. CellWatch allows parents to address some of these concerns even as it exacerbates an age-old parenting tension: We want to keep our children safe, maintain a loving connection and, simultaneously, foster their independence.

“The CellWatch parental control APP provides parents a unique set of tools to make sure their child’s technology is being used in accordance with the parents permissions. The features are optional so the parent can select which features to apply depending on their personal circumstances.”

CellWatch is one of the leading parental assistance apps in the marketplace today. It helps keep the responsible parents informed as to their child’s technology usage.
