Features

What Features We are Providing?

Browser History

Keep an eye on your child’s web surfing activities.

Location Tracking

See the child’s phone location every 5 minutes.

Application Manager

Here the parent can manage all APP’s and functions on the child’s device.

Beacon (Home & Auto)

The home beacon allows the parent to easily turn-off the child’s phone at night (to prevent them from chatting with their friends all night). The Auto beacon prevents texting while driving whenever the vehicle is moving.

Notification

Get notified if your child tries to uninstall the APP or access sites you have forbidden.

Child Phone Data

See the call logs, text messages, email contacts, and social media messages.

Content blocking

The parent is able to block their child’s access to certain sites.

Support

Access the Cell Watch support team 24/7.

CellWatch is one of the leading parental assistance apps in the marketplace today. It helps keep the responsible parents informed as to their child’s technology usage.
